Denzel Washington

What makes a hero?

A hero is someone that we look up to. Sombody that inspires and motivates you to live life. A person that works hard to accomplish their goals and to share that information with the world.

My Hero

Denzel Washington has been a prominet figure in my life. Being an actor, I have seen Denzel in many movies playing many characters. In my opinion, he is easily one of the best actors to be displayed on the big screen. Denzel has even taken part in broadway shows like "Fences" to further his portfolio of achievements. Denzel however, does not limit himself to his acting career. Due to his large status, Denzel has been a figure to many young adults, especially black males. Denzel has also cemented himself as a motivational speaker with his eloquently written speaches. Some of the most notable speaches ocurred at college graduations where he trys to give new graduates advice on the real world. One of his best quotes is "Fail big". I use this quote in my everyday life and use that quote as motivation to give my all in everything I do.
